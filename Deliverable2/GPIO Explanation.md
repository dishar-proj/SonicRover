# The php acts as a secure way to connect devices to the raspberry pi. Connecting directly to the pi could cause various different problems and therefore the php is used as a layer of protection for the pi when connecting devices to the GPIO pins. The diagram we provided details the connection of GPIO2 on the raspberry pi to the GPIO2 pin on the php. The Zener diode in the diagram allows current to flow in both directions and is used as a stable voltage reference. It essentially allows current to flow from the pin on the php to the pin on the raspberry pi while adding a layer of security and stability which protects the pi from having too much power drawn.
